About Us

Hello! My name is Andrew and I live in Maryland with my wife Monica and our hound dog Suzie. I'm currently a Computer Science student, but I have worked in horticulture in the past. My wife and I both love cooking and eating fresh veggies, which inspired us to cultivate a passion for growing some of our own food at home. As of 2021, we have been gardening in one way or another for 3 years. Suzie has only joined our team this year, but she is already the most enthusiastic helper of us all. Her primary responsibilities are keeping morale high and defending the plants from marauding rabbits and squirrels. I hope you enjoy the website and enjoying the picture of Suzie at the bottom of this page.

Please feel free to contact us below with any questions you may have regarding our garden or plans for your garden. I may not have all of the answers, but I can at least try to point you in the right direction. Cheers!
